Mango Sticky Rice

A sweet dessert with sticky rice, coconut milk, and ripe mango.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup glutinous rice
  • 1 ½ cups coconut milk
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 ripe mangoes
Steps:
  1. Soak rice for 4 hours, then steam until soft.
  2. Heat coconut milk with sugar and salt; pour over rice.
  3. Serve with sliced mangoes and drizzle with extra coconut milk.

Green Curry

A fragrant curry made with coconut milk, green chili paste, and fresh basil.

Ingredients:
  • 1 tbsp green curry paste
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 1 cup Thai eggplant
  • Fresh basil leaves
Steps:
  1. Sauté curry paste until fragrant.
  2. Add coconut milk and simmer.
  3. Add chicken and eggplant; cook until tender.
  4. Garnish with basil and serve with rice.

Pad Thai

A popular stir-fried noodle dish with shrimp, tofu, peanuts, and lime.

Ingredients:
  • 200g rice noodles
  • 100g shrimp, peeled
  • 100g tofu, cubed
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Chopped peanuts, lime wedges, and fresh cilantro for garnish
Steps:
  1. Soak the rice noodles in warm water for 30 minutes.
  2. Stir-fry tofu and shrimp in a pan, then add beaten eggs.
  3. Mix in the noodles, fish sauce, sugar, and lime juice.
  4. Garnish with peanuts, cilantro, and lime wedges.

Catfish Salad

A light and spicy salad with grilled catfish, herbs, and a tangy dressing.

Ingredients:
  • 1 lb catfish fillets
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Chopped cilantro, mint, and chili peppers
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
Steps:
  1. Grill catfish and break it into pieces.
  2. In a bowl, mix fish sauce, lime juice, and sugar to make the dressing.
  3. Toss catfish with the dressing, herbs, and cucumber slices.
  4. Serve chilled with additional herbs on top.
